What I Do
Documentary style

I edit documentaries that use interviews, footage, narration, music, and graphics to tell stories. I make them smooth, coherent, authentic, and emotional.

Short form content

I edit short videos for social media, advertising, education, entertainment, and more. I make them engaging and dynamic with fast cuts, transitions, effects, sound, and text.

Music video

I edit music videos that match the song’s rhythm, style, and genre. I use color, lighting, movement, and symbolism to visualize the music. I sync the video and audio and add transitions and effects.

Real estate videoss

I edit real estate videos that show the property’s features, amenities, and value. I use drone footage, gimbal shots, time-lapses, and slideshows to highlight the exterior and interior. I add music, voice-over, text, and graphics.

Animation

I edit animation videos that use computer-generated or hand-drawn images. I work with different animation styles such as 2D, 3D, stop motion, and motion graphics. I add sound effects, music, voice-over, and subtitles.

Corporate video

I edit corporate videos that communicate the company’s or organization’s vision, mission, values, and goals. I show the products, services, features, benefits, and testimonials. I use branding elements such as logos, colors, fonts, and slogans.
